We trust that you've enjoyed your experience with Neo4j and found value in the intuitiveness, speed, and agility of using a native graph database for your applications.

We'd like to invite you to become active in the Neo4j community! If you haven't joined our Community Site and Forum <https://message.neo4j.com/Y0Yem0bN400bD0021RR0kCh> yet, we'd encourage you to do that now. Are you so excited that you want to tell the world about how amazing graph databases are? Request an invite to join our ambassador program <https://message.neo4j.com/z0bD2Y1000eRNnmhR4e0C00>, and share your story for our blog by e-mailing content@neo4j.com <https://message.neo4j.com/ZRNe40hfY0000b0Do201CRm>.
